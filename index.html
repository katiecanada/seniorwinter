Senior Happy Hours

Who says winter quarter isnt the happiest one of all? We're hosting happy hours at cool spots on campus. Come unwind and forget about the winter quarter blues. Great chances to hang out with your friends while doing everything from Karaoke to catching a rooftop view to speed meeting on Valentines to lounging in the March sun.

EANAB and beer/wine specials at all happy hours. Every other Friday starting on Jan 17th. Check out below for details and times.

Happy Hour #1:

Karaoke Night

Only sing in the shower? Talisman level talent? Never sing? Doesn't matter. Come out and either enjoy or make the show with your friends. Perfect way to start your Friday night.

Friday, Jan 17th, 7:30 pm to 9:30 pm @ the Coho. Say you're with '2014 Seniors' for a discount on EANABS, beer, or wine.

Happy Hour #2:

Huang Rooftop

Now that the shiny new Engineering Quad is done, what better way to spend a Friday afternoon than at the very top. Come enjoy the views and conversations with other seniors.

Friday, Jan 31st, 4 pm to 6 pm @ the third floor balcony of Huang. Discounts on EANABS, beer, or wine.

Happy Hour #3:

Valentine's Speed Meet

Want to meet as many classmates as possible before the year ends? This is your chance. We're bringing back the popular speed meet from sophomore year. Who knows? Maybe you'll find a new friend, catch up with an old friend, or meet your future spouse...

Friday, Feb 14th. Time and venue TBA. Specialty hot chocolate bar, desserts, EANABS, beer/wine.

Happy Hour #4:

Treehouse Garden

We're ending the series with a Stanford take on what anyone who traveled abroad to Berlin experienced. Come out and enjoy the (hopefully) warm almost March sun outside of Treehouse while meeting more people. Special section will be blocked off for Stanford seniors.

Friday, Feb 28th. 4 pm to 6 pm @ Treehouse. Discounts on food as well as EANABs, beer, or wine.

Senior Piñata Day

Why? Because who doesn't love pinatas? On this once in a Stanford career experience, we'll be hanging up piñatas all around campus. Armed gaurds will be stationed to make sure only seniors can hit them (okay, so they won't be armed but seriously, only seniors). Take a crack at it on your way to class or track them down to hit them all. You never know what you'll find inside and it's the perfect midterm study break.

11 am to 1 pm on Jan 31st (check this). Map will be released closer to date.
